My First Journey to the CYF London Office

Today was my first day visiting the CodeYourFuture office in London. I was excited to be there, meet my code buddies in person.

Setting Off

Planning my journey was not difficult. Just needed to take the tube from my place and walk some five minutes to arrive at the address. Naturally I used my satnav app for this. Piece of cake, or so I thought.

I started my journey so I could arrive my destination on time. I had to change trains but luckily I did not need to waste time while waiting as my second train arrived right after I got off my first one!

I Got Lost

After getting off the tube at Brixton Station I followed my walking route to the centre. Now, here is where it got tricky. I followed the directions my phone's satnav showed me strictly and, interestingly enough, it led me to an alleyway!

There was only a brickwall on the left side. A lot of noise was coming through the wall and I wondered, Could that be CYF? Is the centre this much active? On the right side a couple of people were unloading a van that blocked the path to my pin on the map, and taking their loads through what appeared to be a side entrance into...I don't know...a grocery store?

I did not want to accept the fact that I was fooled and hoped I might find the entrance to my CYF somewhere on the left side of the alleyway. I resigned when I realised I would hit a dead end, of sorts. The crew working at the van also understood I had lost my way and indicated I could not find any entrance there to whatever they thought I wanted to go to. Of course I made it clear it was not my fault? It was the satnav's, wasn't it?

At this time I was already on the last minutes before my intended 10 am arrival time, so I came out of that alleyway and turned right to start my round-the-corner walk in order to find the real entrance to the center. As soon as I came out of the alleyway I found out it was the gym where all that noise came from. That made sense!

This time I could not let myself tricked again!, I thought, then continued my journey and made sure I came out onto Stockwell Road. Funny I did not think about that in the first place!

Blue Star House

Now I am on the correct side and direction of Stockwell Road, finding door number was my next endeavour.

Hi, Nandos! Do you host CYF somewhere in your property by any chance?

What's that tall building behind you, by the way?

Well, that must be where CYF center is, but how to get in there, umm...

The other place I just passed, where it says 'Blue Star House', cannot be it, can it? It seems to be part of that same building Nandos is though. Alright....

Well, given the property next to Nandos has 246 written on it, I reckon Blue Star House is my best bet. On to Blue Star House, then!
